Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a seat online?

Simply visit our booking system, choose your destination, and follow the step-by-step process.

What if I miss the bus?

If you miss your bus, you can request a refund or reschedule depending on availability.

Can I book multiple seats in a single reservation?

Yes, you can book multiple seats in one reservation! Just select the number of seats during the booking process and provide the necessary passenger details. If you need assistance, feel free to contact our customer service.

Do I need to print my ticket, or can I show the confirmation on my phone?

You can show your receipt to the bus conductor directly from your phone. Printing is optional but can be a hassle. Just ensure your receipt is accessible for a smooth boarding experience.
